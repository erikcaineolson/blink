What is "Blink?"

"Blink" is a basic JavaScript and jQuery function to make stuff appear and disappear on a page. Great for office pranks, confusing your "favorite" web developer, or *shudder* ressurrecting the <blink> tag for HTML5! Blink will not affect <script>, <link> or <style> tags. However... it may try to grab them (in which...maddening...case nothing will blink).

This was made entirely for fun so there are no guarantees!

You simply call Blink(n), where n is an interval in milliseconds (1000n = 1 second).
